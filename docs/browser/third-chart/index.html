Standardized Health and Socio-Economic Indicators for the Top 15 Countries
Chart Type:
Heatmap Chart
The heatmap provides a comparative view of the top 15 countrie's based on their mean health and socio-economic indicators. Each row represents a country, and each column corresponds to an indicator such as "Life expectancy", "Adult Mortality", "Hepatitis B", "Measles", "BMI", "Polio", "Diphtheria", "HIV/AIDS", "GDP", and "Schooling". The color intensity in each cell reflects the countrys standardized score for that indicator, with the color scale provided at the bottom.
